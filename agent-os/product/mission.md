# Product Mission

## Pitch

Educational Marketplace is a freemium learning platform that helps domain experts monetize their knowledge and students access high-quality education by providing a unified marketplace for courses, subscriptions, and digital/physical goods with progress tracking and community engagement.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Domain Experts & Content Creators**: Professionals with specialized knowledge who want to teach, build an audience, and generate revenue through multiple streams (courses, subscriptions, digital/physical goods) without the high commission fees of traditional platforms.
- **Students & Learners**: Individuals seeking credible, niche expertise with hands-on learning, certification opportunities, and direct interaction with instructors.

### User Personas

**The Expert (Content Creator)** (35-45 years old)

- **Role:** Industry professional with 10+ years of specialized experience (engineering, design, business, etc.)
- **Context:** Currently working full-time, wants to build passive income streams and establish thought leadership without building their own website infrastructure
- **Pain Points:** Existing platforms take 50%+ revenue share, limited control over pricing/bundling, can't sell physical goods alongside courses, lack of direct student relationships
- **Goals:** Monetize expertise through multiple revenue streams, build loyal audience through freemium model, retain control over pricing and branding, minimize platform fees

**The Student (Learner)** (25-35 years old)

- **Role:** Early-to-mid career professional seeking to upskill or transition careers
- **Context:** Budget-conscious, wants proof of learning for career advancement, values community and instructor interaction
- **Pain Points:** Hard to assess course quality before purchase, generic platforms lack personalized expert interaction, no unified place to track progress across multiple courses, limited credibility signals
- **Goals:** Find credible experts in niche fields, try courses before committing financially, earn recognized certificates, ask questions and get instructor feedback

## The Problem

### Fragmented Knowledge Monetization

Domain experts face a difficult choice: use high-commission platforms like Udemy (50%+ fees) that limit control, or build custom infrastructure requiring significant technical expertise and upfront investment. Meanwhile, students struggle to find credible niche expertise and meaningful instructor engagement on generic learning platforms.

**Our Solution:** A self-serve marketplace with fair commission rates (10-15%) that enables experts to create courses, manage subscriptions, and sell goods—while students get progress tracking, certifications, and direct access to instructors.

## Differentiators

### Hybrid Monetization Model

Unlike Udemy (course-only) or Patreon (subscription-only), we provide multiple revenue streams in one platform—courses, subscriptions, and digital/physical goods marketplace. This results in higher lifetime value per expert and diversified income opportunities.

### Low Platform Fees with High Control

Unlike traditional platforms taking 50%+ revenue share, we charge 10-15% commission and give experts full control over pricing, bundling, and student relationships. This results in 3-4x more revenue per sale for creators while maintaining platform sustainability.

### Community-First Learning Experience

Unlike passive video platforms, we integrate Q&A systems, reviews, direct messaging, and discussion forums directly into the learning experience. This results in higher course completion rates, better student outcomes, and stronger expert-student relationships.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **Course Builder**: Create structured courses with modules and lessons, set flexible pricing (free, one-time, or subscription), and embed video content from YouTube/Vimeo without hosting costs.
- **Student Enrollment**: Browse courses by category, preview syllabi and instructor bios, complete secure checkout via Stripe, and get instant access to content.
- **Video Player with Progress Tracking**: Watch lessons with automatic progress saving, resume where you left off, and mark lessons complete to track your learning journey.
- **My Courses Dashboard**: View all enrolled courses in one place, see visual progress bars, and quickly jump to your next lesson.
- **Expert Subscriptions**: Subscribe to your favorite instructors to unlock all their paid courses, supporting them with recurring revenue while getting unlimited access to their catalog.

### Collaboration Features

- **Q&A System**: Ask questions on specific lessons and get expert responses in threaded discussions visible to all students.
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Share course experiences with 5-star ratings and written reviews to help future students make informed decisions.
- **Direct Messaging**: Connect privately with instructors for personalized guidance and support.
- **Discussion Forums**: Engage with fellow students in course-specific communities moderated by the expert.

### Advanced Features

- **Assessments & Certification**: Prove your knowledge with auto-graded quizzes and expert-reviewed assignments, earning PDF certificates with instructor signatures to showcase on LinkedIn and resumes.
- **Digital Goods Marketplace**: Purchase downloadable resources (templates, spreadsheets, PDFs, plans) that complement course content for immediate practical application.
- **Physical Goods Store**: Buy instructor-created books, tools, and kits with integrated fulfillment, creating a complete learning ecosystem.
- **Expert Analytics**: Track revenue, student enrollments, completion rates, and engagement metrics to optimize course performance.
- **Watchlist**: Save interesting courses for later to build your personalized learning queue.
